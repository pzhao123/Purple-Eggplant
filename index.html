What is The Eggplant Wars?

The Eggplant Wars is a webcomic created by The Purple Eggplant. It is featured on Discover on Webtoon, a webcomic-publishing platform available to the public. To read it, click here .



Summary

The Eggplant Wars is a fictional story about the species of eggplants fighting a cause. Not just any cause, the cause they are fighting for is eggplant rights! The eggplants want to have a treaty with the humans, but they soon find out that it is harder than they thought!
